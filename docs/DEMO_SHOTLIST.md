# Demo video shot list (~75 seconds)

Record at 1080p+ with system audio. Use the parametric avatar or a model
you have redistribution-safe rights to show; do not show third-party
models of unknown license.

1. (0-8s) Cold open on the fullscreen UI, avatar idling. No narration
   needed; let the breathing/blinking read.
2. (8-25s) Voice conversation. Ask something with a factual answer plus a
   personal detail ("I've been stressing about my thesis defense on
   Friday"). Show her replying aloud, lips moving, text streaming in the
   panel.
3. (25-33s) Interrupt her mid-sentence by speaking. Audio dies instantly,
   the log shows "-- interrupted", she reacts.
4. (33-45s) Quit the app on camera. Show the terminal consolidation line.
   Relaunch. Ask "what do you remember about me?" and let the thesis
   detail come back.
5. (45-55s) Terminal: `python -m amadeus journal`. Scroll her entry about
   the conversation you just had.
6. (55-68s) Terminal: `/recall thesis` in chat mode. Show the score
   breakdown (semantic/keyword/recency/importance) on screen for two
   seconds; this is the shot for technical viewers.
7. (68-75s) Back to the UI. Say something delightful and catch an
   expression trigger. End on her face.

Caption suggestions: keep them factual ("memory consolidation runs on
session end", "hybrid retrieval: vector + BM25 + recency + importance").
No music necessary; her voice is the audio.
